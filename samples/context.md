# Trailhead — sample product brief

**One-liner:** Outreach prioritization for B2B sales teams. Imports your CRM
contacts and surfaces the next 5 people to follow up with each morning.

## Audience

Primary: solo founders running outbound themselves before they have a sales
team. Secondary: sales managers at Series A–B startups (3–8 reps), who often
inherit Trailhead from the founder once the team grows.

## Pricing model

Freemium. Free for the first 100 contacts; $20/seat/month after that. Annual
plans for teams of 5+ at $15/seat/month.

## Where users get stuck (qualitative)

- The first import takes ~10 minutes; founders bounce if they can't paste a CSV.
- The "team workspace" prompt at signup makes solo users feel premature; many
  close the tab and never return.
- Managers can't tell from the dashboard alone whether the AI prioritization
  is good — they want to scroll back through the week and see decisions made.

## Riskiest assumption

That solo-founder workflows generalize to sales-manager workflows. If the
manager use case requires a fundamentally different surface (queues vs. daily
list), the conversion path collapses.

## What "good" looks like

- 60% of new users complete first import within 5 minutes.
- 40% of them invite a teammate within 14 days.
- $50 LTV in the first 90 days for self-serve tier.
